## 1. 저장 + 종료

- 저장 <code class="docutills literal notranslate"><span class="pre">:w</span></code> + <code class="docutills literal notranslate"><span class="pre">enter</span></code>

- 종료 <code class="docutills literal notranslate"><span class="pre">:q</span></code> + <code class="docutills literal notranslate"><span class="pre">enter</span></code>
- 저장후 종료 <code class="docutills literal notranslate"><span class="pre">:wq</span></code> + <code class="docutills literal notranslate"><span class="pre">enter</span></code>
- 저장 안하고 종료 <code class="docutills literal notranslate"><span class="pre">:q!</span></code> + <code class="docutills literal notranslate"><span class="pre">enter</span></code>
